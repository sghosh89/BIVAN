    GA_Anolis_traits.csv
and
    GA_Anolis_MCC.nex
are the raw data and tree from Mahler et al 2013 data deposit:
at https://datadryad.org/resource/doi:10.5061/dryad.9g182

Those authors used a phylogenetic regression to size correct all
    of the data and then applied PCA to the size corrected data.
The file:
    table-s2-ppca.csv
is that PCA downloaded from:
    http://science.sciencemag.org/content/suppl/2013/07/18/341.6143.292.DC1


Those values in GA_Anolis_traits.csv log transformed measurements.


For the sake of simplicity in our data expoloration analyses, MTH
    computed an index for each measurement by subtracting the SVL
    value from every non-SVL character.
This is the log of the length ratio between the trait and the SVL.

    python subtract_SVL_and_to_nexus.py GA_Anolis_traits.csv  >trait_minus_svl.nexus 2>trait_minus_svl_contrasts.csv
    python pic1.py trait_minus_svl.nexus GA_Anolis_MCC.nex  >>trait_minus_svl_contrasts.csv
